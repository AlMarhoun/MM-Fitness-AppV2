# Logo Correction Pass - Options Review

The selected visual reference was treated as the source of truth: a fast handwritten performance signature with white/silver stroke, Ice Blue movement, and a small cyan accent.

## Option 1 - Velocity Signature

- Reference closeness: 9.1/10
- Idea: A fast handwritten MM signature with a soft Ice Blue under-sweep, thin silver main stroke, sharp forward slash, and cyan endpoint.
- Better than previous logo because: It removes the heavy basic monogram feeling and feels more like a premium personal mark.
- Works best: Splash screen, app icon, Home header, navigation active state.

## Option 2 - Apex Flow

- Reference closeness: 8.4/10
- Idea: Taller upward movement with a more expressive signature curve.
- Better than previous logo because: It has more athletic motion and less old-school geometry.
- Works best: Splash screen and large brand moments.
- Weakness: Slightly more decorative at small sizes.

## Option 3 - Private Athlete Script

- Reference closeness: 8.7/10
- Idea: Cleaner signature with a more refined cyan tail detail.
- Better than previous logo because: It feels more luxury and less fitness-template.
- Works best: Header and splash.
- Weakness: App icon version needs more weight to stay legible.

## Option 4 - Ice Blade Signature

- Reference closeness: 8.8/10
- Idea: Thin, sharp, blade-like signature with a strong diagonal stroke.
- Better than previous logo because: It feels faster and more modern.
- Works best: Splash and active navigation.
- Weakness: Slightly too sharp for daily app friendliness.

## Option 5 - Command Signature

- Reference closeness: 8.6/10
- Idea: More stable athletic command-center mark with stronger stroke presence.
- Better than previous logo because: It is more premium and confident.
- Works best: App icon and PWA assets.
- Weakness: Less close to the exact handwritten reference.

## Option 6 - Silver Velocity Mark

- Reference closeness: 8.5/10
- Idea: A cleaner silver-first mark with restrained cyan movement.
- Better than previous logo because: It is calmer and more premium.
- Works best: Light/dark header usage.
- Weakness: Less expressive in splash.

## Selected

Option 1, Velocity Signature, was selected because it is the closest to the reference while staying usable in the app icon, splash screen, header, and navigation active state.
